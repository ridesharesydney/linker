What's being done?

With the announcement on the 4th July 2024, it's still early days, but a small team are currently investigating the options. This may be join a union, it may be a 'strike', for want of a better word - but trust us we will have our voice heard!
For the time being, please join the chat and we'll update you there. Please note, it might take a few days for us to set a few plans into motion and have some discussions with lawyers, unions, etc

How can I help

Our small team is dedicated to finding the way to make our voice heard, but we need some help in certain areas like legal (probono work), publicity/copyright and other key areas. If you think you're able to assist, please let us know via email ridesharesydney@protonmail.com
Please note this service has a harsh spam filter on it, so make sure your seading emails from a real email address.

Why are we using this link page?

Over time, we're needing to communicate important information out to everyone, and eventually one chat group isn't going to be enough! This page will be updated over time with links to important news articles and other information. But in the meantime, above is the link to the Telegram chat!
